About Us

Welcome to Qidame Gebeya, your premier destination for discovering and supporting the vibrant world of Ethiopian commerce. At Qidame Gebeya, we're passionate about connecting individuals, artisans, and businesses with discerning shoppers who appreciate the richness and diversity of Ethiopian products. Our mission at Qidame Gebeya is simple yet profound: to celebrate and elevate Ethiopian craftsmanship, entrepreneurship, and ingenuity. We strive to create a thriving marketplace where local artisans and businesses can thrive while offering customers an unparalleled shopping experience rooted in authenticity, quality, and community. We understand the importance of trust and integrity when it comes to online shopping. That's why we meticulously curate our product offerings, ensuring that each item meets our stringent standards of quality, authenticity, and ethical production. Whether you're searching for unique handcrafted treasures or everyday essentials, you can shop with confidence knowing that every purchase supports local artisans and reflects the rich cultural heritage of Ethiopia.

At the heart of Qidame Gebeya is a commitment to empowering local artisans, small businesses, and entrepreneurs. By providing them with a platform to showcase their talents and products to a global audience, we aim to create economic opportunities, preserve traditional craftsmanship, and foster sustainable livelihoods within Ethiopian communities.

At Qidame Gebeya, we believe in the power of community. We're more than just an e-commerce platform; we're a vibrant community of shoppers, artisans, and enthusiasts who share a passion for Ethiopian culture and craftsmanship. Through social media engagement, forums, and events, we strive to foster meaningful connections, facilitate dialogue, and celebrate the diverse voices and perspectives within our community.

Project Inspiration

The inspiration for this project originated from a personal desire to push my boundaries in client-side functionalities and delve into the realm of modern web development technologies. As an aspiring software engineer, I was keen on exploring the capabilities of frameworks like React, alongside complementary tools such as Tailwind CSS and Supabase. The journey began with a curiosity to understand how these technologies could seamlessly integrate to create robust, interactive web applications. I envisioned a practical project that would not only challenge my technical skills but also serve as a tangible representation of my growth and proficiency in the field. Embarking on this project wasn't merely a requirement from ALX Software Engineering Program; it was an opportunity for self-discovery and skill enhancement. The timeline for the project spanned several weeks, during which I dedicated countless hours to research, experimentation, and development, with the ultimate goal of delivering a polished and functional application.
